01 / About

A researcher bridging machines and minds.

Dr. Faezeh Rohani is an Assistant Professor of Software Engineering at Üsküdar University, where her work explores the interdisciplinary frontier of artificial intelligence and the cognitive sciences.

Her research combines computational methods with insights from neuroscience to advance intelligent systems, healthcare data science, and the analysis of human behaviour — asking how machines can better understand, and serve, the people who use them.

— Positioning

AI × Neuroscience × Human Behaviour — a uniquely interdisciplinary profile with applications in neurotherapy, health data, and cognitive modelling.

— Currently

Teaching, supervising graduate research, and contributing to faculty committees on education, quality assurance, and international exchange.
